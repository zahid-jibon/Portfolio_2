ABOUT ME

Rathendra Nath Paul popularly known as R N Paul was born in Dhaka on April 01, 1973. He completed his graduate (1995) and post graduate (1996) in Accounting from Dhaka University. He also studied Cost and Management Accounting in ICMAB.


He started his career as Management Trainee in RFL Group in 1997 and currently Managing Director of RFL Group, the most diversified and leading conglomerate of Plastics and light engineering products satisfying numerous consumer's various day to day needs at affordable price.


He has been leading RFL Group more than 15 years. For his significant contribution in the economy of Bangladesh by exporting plastics goods to many countries he got CIP (Commercially Important Person) award consecutive four times from the Govt of Bangladesh.
